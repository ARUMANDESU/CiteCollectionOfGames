The Elder Scrolls 5:
Skyrim

Genre: Role-Playing Game
Platform: PC / PlayStation 3 / PlayStation 4 / Xbox 360 / Xbox One / Xbox Series
Release date: 2011.11.11
Publisher: Bethesda Softworks
The developer: Bethesda Softworks

The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim is an action-adventure RPG, the fifth
installment in The Elder Scrolls series, with a huge open world that is
fully explorable from the outset. Players will play the role of
Dragonborn, Dragonborn, who appears in the province of Skyrim
exactly during the return of the legendary dragons, which, according
to legend, are going to destroy the world. Dragonborn will have to
overcome many difficulties on the way to his goal - to stop the dragon
lord Alduin, who is preparing to unleash his rage on the whole world.



Players are free to choose whether to go through the storyline, do side
quests, or just wander the world. In Skyrim there is no clear concept
of "character class" - the protagonist can be developed as the player
likes, creating all sorts of combinations of standard "role-playing"
classes.
