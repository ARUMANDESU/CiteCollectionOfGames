Grand Theft Auto 5

Genre: Action, Shooter
Platform: PC / PlayStation 3 / PlayStation 4 / PlayStation 5 / Xbox 360 / Xbox One / Xbox Series
Release date: 2013.9.17
Publisher: 2K Games
The developer: Rockstar Games

Grand Theft Auto V (aka GTA 5 or GTA V) is the fifteenth game in the
Grand Theft Auto series, made in the Action / Sandbox genre. For the
first time in the history of the series, there are three main characters
in the game at once, between whom you can switch almost at any
time. The player is given complete freedom of action - he can travel
around the city and its environs, rob passers-by, steal cars - or live the
simple life of an ordinary person. Each of the heroes has its own
unique characteristics - one can slow down time while driving, the
second can slow down time in shootouts, and the third can become a
mad berserker, increasing the damage done to enemies.



The setting for Grand Theft Auto V is the fictional city of Los Santos,
which first appeared in one of the previous games in the series, Grand
Theft Auto: San Andreas. The game takes place in 2013, when former
friends and bank robbers Trevor and Michael meet again. The main
goal of the player is to encircle the US Federal Vault, taking with him
more than two hundred million. On the way to this goal, friends will
face many obstacles that will lead to one of three endings - which
was not previously seen in any part of the series.
